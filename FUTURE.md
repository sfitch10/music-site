## New Features
- **Critic/Crowd Divide Leaderboard** — Rank albums by the size of their Stage/Crowd gap.
- **Genre Health Index** — Which genres are scoring highest right now? Trend view over time.
- **Publication Bias Tracker** — Which outlets consistently score above or below the combined average?
- **The Snub Report** — Albums the crowd loves that critics ignored, and vice versa.
- **Where Does It Land?** — Head-to-head album comparison tool.
- **Decade Showdowns** — Best albums of the 2010s vs 2020s, scored by the same methodology.
- **Hall of Fame** — Albums that have held top tier for 12+ consecutive months.
- **Aging Curve** — Visualization of how an album's score changes over time as crowd interest shifts.
- **Foundation catalog** — Expand to all-time albums (pre-2020), scored retroactively.
- **User accounts and verified critic program** — Optional future expansion; currently intentionally excluded.
- **Score live-updating ticker** — Real-time score updates for On Deck albums without page reload.
- **Artist pages** — Aggregate view of all albums by a single artist with career arc visualization.
- **Weekly digest email** — Opt-in newsletter summarizing score changes and new On Deck graduations.
